day2
Skalnaté Pleso — Gondola Up
Wed Aug 12 · 15 min drive · gondola to 1751m
must-docable carmountain lake

Enclosed gondola (safe for Damian) to an alpine lake under Lomnický štít. Kids under 6 ride free.

7:00Early breakfast
8:00Park at P+R Eurocamp (free)
8:30Gondola up
9:00At the lake. Walk the loop, photos, spot chamois
11:00Gondola down → lunch in Lomnica
16:00TANAP Museum — wildlife dioramas
Pack warm. 10–14°C at the top even in August. Jackets + long pants. Leave stroller, use carrier.

Tickets: gopass.travel — buy early, dynamic pricing. Adults ~25–40€. Kids under 6: free.

Foggy morning? Skip this, do a castle instead. Check the webcam at 8 AM.
day3
Kežmarok Castle + UNESCO Church
Thu Aug 13 · 25 min drive · easy half-day
castleUNESCO

Castle right in town, no climbing needed. And a wooden church built in 1717 without a single nail. kezmarok.com

9:45Castle — guided ~80 min. Tower, armory, chapel
11:30Wooden church (5 min walk) — plain outside, jaw-dropping inside
12:00Town square, lunch, ice cream
13:30Back to hotel → siesta → playground

If Damian gets restless during the tour, Katka takes him to the courtyard. Pavel + Elena finish tower + armory.

Entry: adults 5€, child 3-6y 1€. Family ~11€.

day4
Train + Tricklandia + Tatrabob
Fri Aug 14 · triple feature
Damian's favetrainbobsled

Historic Tatra Electric Railway (since 1908), an optical illusion gallery, and a bobsled run.

9:20Train → Starý Smokovec. Look for Kométa (1913) or Trojča (1969)
10:00Tricklandia — 500m² trick-art, AR, mirror maze, anti-gravity room
12:00Train back → hotel → siesta
15:45Tatrabob — Elena + parent, 3 rides. Damian can't ride (under 5)

Train: kids under 6 free, ~2€/adult. Tricklandia: ~37€ family, no strollers. Tatrabob: ~3-5€/ride.

Works in light rain. Tricklandia is indoor, train runs regardless. Tatrabob won't run wet — reschedule that part.
day5
Popradské Pleso — Lake Hike
Sat Aug 15 · 25 min drive + 4 km paved trail
must-dostroller okglacial lake

Paved trail the whole way. Restaurant right at the lake with mountain views.

7:00Early breakfast (Saturday — go early)
8:40Start hike — blue trail through forest
10:00Popradské Pleso. Skipping stones, photos, chamois-spotting
11:30Lunch at Chata Kapitána Morávku
12:30Walk down (~50 min) → hotel → siesta

4 km one way (8 total) · +300m elevation · paved · up 75-90 min · down 45-60 min

Damian rides the stroller. Elena walks — motivate with "lunch at the lake restaurant."

Rain = skip. Trail gets muddy, lake in fog is pointless. Swap with rest day.
day6
Rest Day
Sun Aug 16 · no car, no plan, no stress
recharge

After 5 active days everyone needs a reset. Sunday = packed attractions everywhere else. Stay at the hotel, do whatever you feel like.

  • Mini-zoo, pony rides
  • Pool + jacuzzi
  • Saunas (take turns)
  • Animation program, kids' cinema
  • Billiards, darts, table tennis
  • Walk around Gerlachov — meadows, stream, wildflowers
Tired kids = meltdowns. A rest day now means the last 3 days go smooth.

Mon Aug 17 · 50 min drive · full day in Liptov
must-dolive craftshorses

30+ historic buildings. Blacksmith, potter, weaver working live. Hucul horses. Miniature train through the grounds. skanzenpribylina.sk

9:30Guided tour: Gothic church + Renaissance manor
10:15Free roam — houses, crafts, animals
11:30Train ride (Damian's second favourite thing after ponies)
12:00Horse ride for Elena
13:00Lunch in Liptovský Mikuláš
14:00Optional: Vlkolínec (UNESCO) or Bricklandia — or just head back

Entry: adults 3€, kids basically free. Family ~7.50€. Stroller-friendly. Free parking.

day8
Stará Ľubovňa Castle
Tue Aug 18 · 45 min drive · last big trip
must-docastlepanorama

Biggest castle of the trip. Hilltop fortress, panoramic Tatra views, 25-building open-air museum below. hradlubovna.sk

Split up

9:30Pavel + Elena → Castle (90 min — tower, armory, torture chamber, 46m well)
9:30Katka + Damian → Museum (flat, stroller OK, animals, benches)
11:00Meet up, explore museum together
12:30Lunch → drive back → siesta
18:00Last dinner at hotel. Start packing after

Family combo: castle + museum = 26€. Photos free.

Daily pattern

6–11 AMUsually clear. Best time for everything.
11 AM–2 PMClouds build. Getting overcast.
2–6 PMThunderstorms likely (30-60 min).
6 PM+Clears up. Good sunsets.
Main activities before noon. Siesta during storms. Gondolas shut down in lightning — check status each morning.

What to wear

Layering

  • Base: T-shirt
  • Mid: Fleece or hoodie
  • Shell: Packable rain jacket
  • Legs: Shorts + long pants (bring both)
  • Feet: Hiking boots + sandals
25°C in the valley can feel like 5°C at Skalnaté Pleso with wind. Always bring a jacket to the mountains.
